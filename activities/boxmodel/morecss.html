  1. What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

    2. Answer: Block tags can or contain div,header,nav, main, footer, h1 - h6, and p. Width and height can be adjust and add marigin, padding and border. while Inline tags has no line break and we can't adjust the size and move them. It can be used in span,a, em, strong, mark and img.

  2. What is the margin, border, and padding?

    3. Answer: Margin is the space outside an element's border that pushes it away from surrounding elements. Border is the line that surrounds an element, separating its content and padding from the margin. Padding is the space inside an element, between the content and the border. When you increase the padding, the content moves inward, creating extra space between the text and the border.
