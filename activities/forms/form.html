Web Design

  1. The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do?

    2. Answer: tag specifies the URL where the form data will be sent after submission.

  2. The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do?

    3. Answer: attribute defines how form data is sent to the server. The two most common values are post and get

  3. What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"?

    4. Answer: A GET request sends data in the URL, like when you search for something on Google and see the search terms in the address bar. A POST request sends data in the background, like when you log in to a website, and your password is sent securely without showing in the URL.
