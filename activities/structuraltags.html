Structural Tags

1. Header: To help your SEO. used to hold introductory content or navigation links for a webpage or a section.

2. Nav: The navigation with link to other sites of your own. Used to hold the main navigation links of your website.

3. Main: Contains the main content of your webpage. It is where you put the information or functionality that is most important to your page.

4. Footer: Holds information that usually appears at the bottom of the page. Usually contains contact details, social media links, and copyrights
