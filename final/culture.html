Kapre: A Mythical Creature of The Philippines

The Kapre is a mythical creature in Philippine folklore, often depicted as a giant, dark-skinned, and hairy being that lives in large trees, particularly balete (banyan) trees. It is known to smoke a large cigar and has glowing red eyes.

Characteristics of Kapre:

  • Tall and muscular (some stories claim it reaches 7 to 9 feet in height).
  • Lives in trees, particularly ancient and massive ones.
  • Smokes cigars or tobacco that never burns out.
  • Tricks or scares people, but is generally not violent.
  • Can become invisible to humans unless it wants to be seen.
  • Some stories say a Kapre can befriend humans or even fall in love with them.

Kapre in Filipino Culture:

  • TUsed to scare children into coming home before dark.
