My Hero - Mylene Naingue

Profile Page Hero Page

Mylene Villarosa Naingue is my mother, and I consider her my hero because she is one of the people who raised me into the person I am today. She graduated with a degree in Accountancy and is a mother of five children. She continues to inspire her children to always do better. Her mere existence is what drives me to go beyond my limits. She is also one of the people who have helped me develop skills such as photography.

Three things that would best described her as Hero:

Selfless
She always prioritize her family before herself and she always find ways to make us happy
Responsible
She is a responsible mother that whenever one of her children is sick, she automatically make ways to get us better.
Understanding
Despite of having five children with different attitude she strives ti find common ground to connect with us and to understand us in a way she can relate with our emotions.
