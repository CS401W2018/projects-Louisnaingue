My Hero - Karl Patacsil

Interests Page Hero Page

Karl Patacsil is a renowned Filipino portrait photographer and filmmaker. Most of his photographs and films are taken in Baguio. He became popular by posting videos of himself offering free photoshoots to random people. His work and skills in photography are excellent. He has now millions followers on his social media platforms. His excellency and consistency to his works what insipres me to go beyond limits.

Three things that would best described Him (Hero):

Persistent
His determination to continue to learn and dig deeper into his art photography from just being a beginner to known photographer in the Philipines.
Adaptability
Karl adapts the use of social media platforms in his work. His ability to adjust to the environment of this generation is what makes him stand out.
Passion
His deep love for capturing authentic moments and empowering others through his creative work keeps him motivated.
