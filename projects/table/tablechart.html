These are the list of top ten highest grossing films of all time. It reflects the evolution of the movie industry throughout time. Avatar (2009) remains the highest-grossing film. Avengers: Endgame (2019) came close to surpassing it, riding the wave of Marvels interconnected cinematic universe. Titanic (1997), once the highest-grossing film, still holds a top spot due to its emotional storytelling and historical significance. Like the other films, this tells how people love to watch films that connects with the audience or give a whole new aspects of life.
